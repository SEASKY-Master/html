================================
无线HID模块设计(ESP32S3-BLE-HID)
================================

+----------+------------+
|   数据   |    说明    |
+==========+============+
| byte0    | 帧头(0XA5) |
+----------+------------+
| byte1    | 数据帧长度 |
+----------+------------+
| byte2    | report_id  |
+----------+------------+
| byte3-11 | buff[8]    |
+----------+------------+
| byte12   | 固定0XFE   |
+----------+------------+
